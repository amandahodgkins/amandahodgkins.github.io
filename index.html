Intro to Digital Fabrication

Intro to Digital Fabrication is a class at Wheaton College designed to familiarize students with technology by having them apply it in a series of hands-on projects. This website showcases my work! Enjoy!

Assignments

Week 1: Project Management

Design a website

Week 2: Laser Cutting

Make a box with a living hinge

Week 3: Additive Manufacturing

Design and 3D print a token

Week 4: CAD, CAM, Plants, and Prusas

Print a vase and design a watering can

Week 5: Subtractive Manufacturing

Learn to use the Axiom CNC and create something

Week 6: Slicer Mini

Laser cut a 3D cardboard object

Week 7: Slicer Big

Laser cut a larger version of your object

Week 8: Vinyl Cutting

Create a graphic for a sweatshirt, cut, and apply it.
