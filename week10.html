Week 10: Electronics 2

The assignment for week 10 was to practice circuits and coding Arduinos in Tinkercad.

Video 1

Video 1 went over the basics of making circuits in Tinkercad with Arduinos. I put an led on the breadboard and added the proper resistor in order for the light to turn on when I started the simulation.

I also learned about using buttons and a potentiometer.

Video 2

Video 2 went over coding the Arduino. I learned the various functions that you could creat and show using the serial monitor. The modulo function shows the value of the remainder after division.

I also learned about if statements

After learning the basics, I practiced with my own code to get comfortable using it. The one in the serial monitor shows that the statement that the distance is greater than the steps is true.
